About Me

Hello everyone I’m Josh Carter I’m 36 years old and live in Key West, FL. I was raised in Southern Illinois and Southern Indiana and moved back down to Key West in 2009 and have been here since. I worked as a Craps dealer for Harrah’s casino for a few years, ran catamaran trips in Key West taking large groups out to the reef 7 miles offshore for 2 ½ years, and I’ve been a personal trainer since 2015. I’ve gotten to a point where my current job isn’t much of a challenge anymore and I started looking into coding and became very intrigued with it and decided that it was the right time to make a career change. I believe that we can learn anything as long as we apply ourselves and keep an open mind. I’m very excited about the experience at hand.
